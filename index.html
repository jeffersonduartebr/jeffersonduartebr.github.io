Paper Summarizer public jeffersonduartebr/Paper_summarizer ↗

Generates a thesis-ready comparative chapter from a corpus of scientific PDFs. Native text extraction with OCR fallback, GPU-aware chunking sized to available VRAM, and synthesis with a local LLM.

Python · Ollama · PyMuPDF · pytesseract · CUDA

Document Chat & PRISMA Review public jeffersonduartebr/document_chat ↗

Streamlit application for multi-document Q&A and one-click generation of PRISMA-style systematic reviews. Two-layer caching (in-memory plus on-disk) and GPU-accelerated inference inside Docker.

Streamlit · Docling · Ollama · Docker · NVIDIA CUDA
